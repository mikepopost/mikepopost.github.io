(Pronounced: Poe-Kriff-Key)

Mike “Po” Pokryfke is an Emmy Award-winning editor and producer with over a decade of experience in a wide variety of roles on set and in post. He’s worked on short films, music videos, live events and commercials in the Chicago area. He loves coffee, Twizzlers and justice. He usually gets too much of the first, wisely avoids the second, and rarely gets the third. He hates tomatoes and writing about himself in the third person. He is a father of 3 (2 boys and a girl, all under the age of 7) and thus prone to little sleep.

Mike graduated from DePaul University’s School of Computing and Digital Media with a BA in Digital Cinema, where his primary focus was on production lighting and cinematography. In the years since, he has devoted most of his time to the post-production side of filmmaking, learning valuable skills online and on-the-job.

After working freelance for a few years, Mike landed a job creating commercials for WGN-TV’s multicast channels, THIS-TV and Antenna TV, where he won an Emmy for his promo work. From there, he worked with FOUR Productions as well as WGN Radio, creating video content for clients. In both roles, Mike served as a main point-of-contact developing and pitching concepts, while also handling planning and execution. His jack-of-all-trades skill set allowed him to serve as producer, director, camera operator, gaffer, sound recorder, grip, editor, and motion graphics editor across a multitude of projects.

In January of 2020, Mike joined the cable network WGN America, where he handled the promotion of the network’s original series "Almost Paradise." After one season, the network pivoted to a primetime news format launching "NewsNation." Mike currently works at Weigel Broadcasting, where he oversees the writing, production, and editing of commercials for the Story Television network. He also pitches in creating promos for Weigel's other brands, MeTV, H&I, START, Dabl, and more.

Mike also put googly eyes on Lady Edith from Downton Abbey a few times.
